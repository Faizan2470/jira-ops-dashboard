TitleKiosk Deal Creation Blocked for $0 Combo Items with Required Price Modifiers
Affected RestaurantBuzz & Brew Coffee
Location ID1883
Restaurant ID1850
EnvironmentProd
PriorityHigh
Description

We created a combo item with a $0 base price and required priced modifiers. The required modifiers include Pastry Choice and Latte Flavors, which determine the final item price.

  • On the POS, we initially attempted to handle this through a comp in Menu Manager. However, because the combo item’s base price is $0, the comp does not apply as expected. We have identified a workaround for the POS.
  • On the Kiosk & Online, we attempted to create a deal for the same combo item, but the system does not allow the deal to be created because the base item price is not greater than $1. This restriction is blocking deal creation even though the final item price exceeds $1 after the required priced modifiers are selected.

Expected behavior: The Kiosk & Online should allow deal creation based on the final calculated item price, including required priced modifiers, rather than only validating against the $0 base price.
